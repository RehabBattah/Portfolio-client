About me

I am a UI/UX designer focused on turning ideas into clear, human‑centered experiences and intuitive user flows. My approach blends research, empathy, and visual craft to create products that feel effortless and consistent across platforms.

I specialize in user research and personas, information architecture, wireframes, and interactive prototypes. I design and maintain scalable design systems, apply accessibility standards (WCAG), and deliver polished UI in Figma and Adobe XD with clean typography, color, and spacing tokens.

I collaborate closely with stakeholders and engineers, iterate through usability testing and analytics, and document handoff details for smooth implementation. My goal is to ship experiences that are usable, inclusive, and delightful.
